Family Trip with the Matriarch

A couple years ago, my grandmother received knee surgery and was feeling a little down. My family decided to lift her spirits by setting up a trip to Japan, one of her favourite destinations. We wanted to provide a change of scenery and plan some quality time with the extended family.

This map shows the cities and key memories made in Japan.

Tokyo

Our first stop was Tokyo, the capital of Japan. Most of Japan is a temperate climate, which allows the country to have four distinct seasons. We spent some time appreciating the colourful foliage of the Japanese gardens. The ginko trees sport yellow fan-shaped leaves, and the well-known Japanese maple have seven tipped leaves of a vibrant red hue. The garden we visited had a traditional pond with koi fish, or Japanese ornamental carp.
